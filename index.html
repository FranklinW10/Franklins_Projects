About Me

I am a junior at Drake University pursuing a triple major in Computer Science, Mathematics, and Data Analytics, with a minor in Physics. I enjoy working on projects that sit at the intersection of computation and physical science, including simulation, machine learning, and data analysis.

Outside of academics I have worked on research with Drake University's MISSFIT computational group, where I contributed to a particle tracking simulation written in C that models radiation interactions with spacecraft.

I am always looking for new opportunities to apply my skills — feel free to reach out via email or LinkedIn.
